## 4 Weeks Before

- [ ] Get your free moving quote
- [ ] Book your moving date
- [ ] Start decluttering — donate, sell, or toss what you don't need
- [ ] Notify your landlord (if renting)
- [ ] Begin researching schools, utilities, and services at your new location

---

## 2 Weeks Before

- [ ] Start packing non-essentials (seasonal items, books, decor)
- [ ] Label every box by room
- [ ] Confirm parking and elevator access at both locations
- [ ] Forward your mail (USPS change of address)
- [ ] Transfer or set up utilities at your new address
- [ ] Notify insurance, banks, subscriptions of your address change

---

## 1 Week Before

- [ ] Pack most of your home — leave out only daily essentials
- [ ] Prepare an essentials bag (toiletries, phone charger, change of clothes, medications, important documents)
- [ ] Defrost and clean your refrigerator
- [ ] Confirm your move date and arrival time with us
- [ ] Measure doorways and hallways at your new home for large furniture

---

## Moving Day

- [ ] Do a final walkthrough of your old home — check closets, cabinets, attic, garage
- [ ] Point the crew to items needing special care
- [ ] Keep your essentials bag with you (not on the truck)
- [ ] Be available by phone in case the crew needs to reach you
- [ ] Do a walkthrough at your new home before the crew leaves

---

## After the Move

- [ ] Unpack essentials first (kitchen, bathroom, bedroom)
- [ ] Check all items against your inventory
- [ ] Update your driver's license and vehicle registration
- [ ] Meet your neighbors
- [ ] Leave us a review if we earned it
