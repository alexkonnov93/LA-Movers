## FAQ Section

**Q: How long does packing take?**
A: A typical 2-bedroom home takes 3–4 hours with a 2-person packing crew. Larger homes may take a full day.

**Q: Can I pack some things myself?**
A: Absolutely. Many customers pack clothes and personal items, then have us handle fragile and heavy items.

**Q: Is packing included in the moving price?**
A: Basic furniture wrapping and protection is always included. Full packing services are an add-on — ask for pricing when you request your quote.
