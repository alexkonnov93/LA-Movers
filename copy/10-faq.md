## General

**Q: Are you licensed and insured?**
A: Yes. We are fully licensed by the California Public Utility Commission and carry general liability, cargo insurance, and workers compensation on every job. CA License #[insert].

**Q: How do I get a moving estimate?**
A: Fill out our online form, call (323) 813-7177, or request a virtual walkthrough. Quotes are free, no-obligation, and usually ready in minutes.

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**
A: 2–4 weeks for local moves, 4–6 weeks for long distance. We accommodate last-minute and same-day moves when crews are available.

**Q: Do you move on weekends and holidays?**
A: Yes. We offer weekend availability. Holiday availability may vary — contact us to check.

**Q: Do you use subcontractors?**
A: Never. Every mover is a trained, full-time member of our team.

## Pricing

**Q: Are there hidden fees?**
A: No. Your quote includes truck, fuel, blankets, padding, shrink wrap, furniture assembly, and all equipment. We don't charge extra for stairs, long walks, or travel time.

**Q: Do you charge for travel time?**
A: No. The clock starts when we arrive at your door, not when we leave our warehouse.

**Q: Do you charge for breaks?**
A: Every minute of a break is deducted from the total time. You only pay for the time we work.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Cash, credit/debit cards, and certified checks. Payment is due upon completion. Cash discount available — ask when you quote.

## During the Move

**Q: Do I need to be present?**
A: We recommend it at pickup and delivery to direct placement. A designated representative can take your place if needed.

**Q: What happens if something gets damaged?**
A: We take every precaution — individual wrapping, professional padding, secure loading. In the rare event of damage, contact us within 48 hours. Our claims process is straightforward and fair.

**Q: Do you disassemble and reassemble furniture?**
A: Yes. Included with every move at no extra charge.

**Q: Do I have to tip the movers?**
A: Tips are always appreciated but never required.

## Packing & Preparation

**Q: Do you offer packing services?**
A: Yes — full-service, partial, or fragile-only. We supply all materials.

**Q: What can't you transport?**
A: Hazardous materials, perishable food, flammables, or live plants on long-distance moves. Valuables like jewelry and important documents should travel with you.

**Q: How should I prepare for moving day?**
A: Clear walkways, label boxes by room, set aside essentials you'll need immediately, and confirm parking/elevator access at both locations.
